What IS the Correct Price?

This analysis focuses on a rideshare app that faces quite the conundrum: They are attempting to optimize their profits for a 1-year time period, but are finding that as they take a higher margin from their drivers, their drivers are much less likely to actually pick up riders. Riders who are not getting picked up by drivers (or are otherwise being cancelled by their drivers) are then churning and no longer utilizing their app.

This analysis focuses on rectifying this issue by examining the lifetime value associated with the various rider types (those who experience a matching issue and those who do not) in order to determine the optimal margin for the rideshare company to take.
