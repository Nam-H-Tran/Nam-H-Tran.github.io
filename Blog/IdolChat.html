IdolChat - The Ultimate K-Pop Fanatic App

Before jumping right into this, one should be familiar with both K-Pop and Idol culture in Asia. K-Pop stands for "Korean Pop", a style of music that has been exploding in popularity. Nowadays, K-Pop groups such as Blackpink have headlining performances at Coachella, showcasing the rising popularity of K-Pop. Idols are celebrities that work in the field of K-Pop, generally under a heavily manufactured image for maximum appeal. These idols are a massive deal as these celebrities not only have a large amount of followers (Lisa of Blackpink has 94million+ followers on Instagram alone), but fans maintain a sort of parasocial relationship with these idols, due to the aforementioned manufactured image of each of these idols.

In order to capitalize on such a relationship, I am proposing the utilization of generative AI in the form of a text chat window. The idol would then take the form of a chatbot. This would allow users to "converse" with their favorite idol, without requiring active input from the idol at all times. It will require a large language model in order to capture the detail of how the idol is likely to respond to any particular comment, as well as general safeguards in place to prohibit illicit or otherwise illegal messages to be sent from the chatbot. This type of material would likely utilize an API connection to ChatGPT or similar technology, but for the purposes of an initial examination on if this idea would even work, let's avoid getting into the technical details.

From here, let's proceed with a basic wireframing exercise to explore how this idea would work. The purposes of a wireframe exercise is to capture the very basic level of what goes into an idea, hence we don't attempt to capture final aesthetics or technical scopes in this. What I am attempting to do is to provide just enough information that anyone would be able to look at the wireframe and understand what idea I've come up with so that they can run with it (designers, engineers, etc.). So with all of this foundational information out of the way, let's get on it!
