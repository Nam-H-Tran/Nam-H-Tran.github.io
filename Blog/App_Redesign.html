Let's Talk About User Experience

The user experience is a core component of what makes apps, well, work. Designing for user experiene relies on having a deep understanding of your users, what they need, value, abilities, and limitations. Just simply listing it out, it's clear to tell it is not a straight forward process and contains many different components.

In this study, there is an application (FindFood) that is struggling with their present user experience design and converting it to actual usage from their customers. I go through an actual process of how I would go through and redesign the application in order to encourage use, including modification of different functionalities of the application.
