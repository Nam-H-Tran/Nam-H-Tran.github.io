More About Me

Hi there! My name is Nam H. Tran, and I am a highly skilled project manager with experience in managing end-to-end project lifecycles for software development and implementation projects. I've developed my skills from a variety of areas: My bachelor/masters degree in aerospace engineering; my ongoing MBA program; roles as a Systems Analysis Engineer and Project Management; to various hobbyist projects of mine (including 3D Printing and cloud server hosting for my Dungeons and Dragons games). I have experience managing projects in both waterfall and agile methodologies and pride myself on honest, open communication to stakeholders, customers, and my own technical resources.

I believe in always continuing to learn and develop new skills over time. To this effect, this website you're viewing right now is an example of such. I have acquired the following certifications: Certified SolidWorks Associate (CSWA), Lean Six Sigma Green Belt, and Project Management Professional (PMP). I view growth and development to be a critical part of who I am as a person, and I know I would certainly be bored if I wasn't always trying to find something new and exciing to do!

In my free time, you'll find me playing Dungeons and Dragons, writing, reading, hiking, enjoying time with my friends, and doing overly complex analyses of some of my favorite media. I hope that you'll join me on this adventure and that we both get to learn something new from each other!
